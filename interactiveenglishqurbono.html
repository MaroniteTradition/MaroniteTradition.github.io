Classic Syro-Maronite Qurbono Study Tool

Information

Last updated: 2021-05-01 7:48:50 p.m.

This tool is for study purposes only, for those who want to study the pre-1993 form of the Syro-Maronite Qurbono "Mass" and are unable to read Syriac nor Arabic. Audio samples are also available for some sections. This tool is a work in progress and is not finished. No Anaphora has been included, that is the next phase of the project. These translations were not approved for public use: private study use only.

The 1953 Sfeir booklet was not intended to be a complete translation, some prayers are missing.

The 1967 El-Hayek seems like an experimental translation.
